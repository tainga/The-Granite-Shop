The Granite and Marble Shop is conveniently located on the White Horse Pike six miles northwest of Atlantic City, at 400 E. Absecon Blvd., Absecon NJ 08201. Click here to view directions on Google maps.

Coming from the North:

  1. Follow Route 30 / White Horse Pike towards Atlantic City.
  2. After crossing Station Avenue, take the jug handle next to The Home Depot and Staples and make a U-turn.
  3. Follow 0.3 miles along E. Absecon Blvd. The Granite and Marble Shop will be on your right. Parking and office entrance are located at the back of the building.

Coming from Atlantic City:

  1. Head Northeast along Route 30 / Absecon Blvd.
  2. Drive 6 miles until reaching The Granite and Marble Shop on your right. Parking and office entrance are located at the back of the building.
